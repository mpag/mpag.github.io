CONCEPT FACADE DESIGN
Dynamic Axonometric

The STEM School Inquiry Hub Facade is a prefabricated, energy-positive, and visually dynamic cladding system. Containing 720 panels, 6 panels types, 4 colours and over 600m2 of glazing, the facade design seeks to engage and excite students with it's forward-facing design. Prefabricated offsite, and rapidly assembled at speed over over 200m2 per/day, this facade complements it's formal complexity with on-site efficiency.
