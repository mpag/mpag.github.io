The Idea

  'The aim of the new building is not to increase classrooms or student numbers, but instead to respond to the new model of teaching. The new building also houses the staff areas and the library.
   By combining all spaces into one building, the planning becomes more efficient and the students can access all learning resources without have to move from building to building. Staff also have a sense of ownership over their teaching area'

  Phillip Rossington, Principal, BVN
